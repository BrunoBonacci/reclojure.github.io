reClojure

    Virtual Conference

    December 2-3, 2022

    Given the succesful 2021 edition, reClojure 2022 is going to be virtual again! Mark the date! reClojure is a free, community driven conference that brings together inspiring speakers in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    The theme of this year's conference is Clojure Growth: how can we increase Clojure popularity and get more programmers to join our fantastic community?

    The CfP isn't open yet. In the meanwhile, we'd like to get in touch with anyone who has little or no experience presenting in public but has something interesting to share. We'd like to help getting your voice out there! Please get in touch with organizers@reclojure.org to discuss ideas and work together.
